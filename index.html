Hello. I'm Ken.

Front-End Web Developer Extraordinaire

Who I Am

I’m a designer turned developer based in Salt Lake City. Since dedicating myself to code, I’ve gained over 5 years of real world experience, built and maintained a variety of front end web solutions for local and global scale clients, and worked alongside some of the most talented people in the industry.

What I Do

While I still harbor delusions of design abilities, I tend to stick with what I do best. What I do best is create beautiful, efficient, responsive websites and web applications. I merge clean, semantic HTML, thoughtful CSS, and efficient, readable Javascript to create elegant and powerful user experiences.

Why I'm Different

Anyone can learn the basics of web development. Being able to always make things work is a gift. Web browsers, devices, and budgets have their limitations, but there is always a way to innovate and provide effective, feasible solutions. That’s why you need me. I find those solutions.
